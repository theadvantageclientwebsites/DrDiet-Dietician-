Theme Analysis for DrDiet

What you already have

  • Theme 1 (Clinical Teal) matches your live theme.ts — solid healthcare baseline
  • Themes 2–5 cover SaaS navy, nutrition green, dark mode, and warm terracotta
  • Themes 6–16 offer light/soft alternatives for all-day comfort
  • All follow the proven pattern: dark or tinted sidebar + light content area

What's missing (now added)

  • DrDiet Signature — refined evolution of Theme 1 with sage-green nutrition accents
  • Teal + Amber — clinical trust + warm energy (ideal for Indian wellness brands)
  • Forest Sage Pro — premium holistic nutrition clinic feel
